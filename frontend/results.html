Core Platform Capabilities

Spatial Discovery

Interactive GIS map click-to-point and area selection for 5km boundary marking. Real-time coordinate capture auto-feeds the ML pipeline.

Predictive AI Models

Random Forest regressors trained on NGCM geochemical database (10,004 records, 68 elements) for mineral probability and concentration scoring.

Automated Reports

Instantly compiled PDF reports with mineral inventory, latitude/longitude/altitude, geological zone, nearest deposits, and XAI model interpretation.

Prospectivity Heatmaps

Dynamic density heatmaps generated from mineral occurrence registry. Color scale from blue (low) to red (high potential) across the exploration region.

Geochemical Database

Spatial KNN join against NGCM stream sediment records for local background geochemistry. Lithology intersected from 3,599 GSI shapefile polygons.
